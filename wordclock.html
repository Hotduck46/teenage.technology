to install word clock on macOS for safari:

decompress the word clock file by double clicking on it,
then drag the "word clock.app" into your applications folder.
next, control-click (right click) on "word clock.app", and click "open anyway" (if prompted)
to launch word clock and follow the steps to install it as a safari extension.

to install word clock on chrome, edge, opera, or brave:

download the respective file for your browser,
then open your extensions page on chrome, edge, opera, or brave.
(this is typically done by typing your browser name, then ://extensions, as in chrome://extensions)

in chrome,
enable developer mode (temporarily) and unzip the "word clock chrome.zip" file.
next, click load unpacked and select the word clock chrome folder you unzipped.
you can disable developer mode once word clock is installed.
chrome might show the alert "Chrome can't verify where this extension comes from"
but you can silence it by clicking the three dots next to the alert and click "keep this extension".

for opera, brave, and edge:
drag and drop the word clock file you downloaded into the extensions page.
turn the word clock on, pin it to your toolbar for the best experience, and tell the time with a click.
